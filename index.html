About Me

I am an Oracle Fusion HCM Cloud Technical Consultant, currently working as a Senior Developer at Cognizant. I have 6+ years of end-to-end experience in Global HR, Learning Cloud and Talent Management modules with expertise in Integrations and Reporting.

I have an instinct for good design and a strong desire to self-improve. When I am not consulting with customers solutioning their HR Transformation projects, I like to learn new technologies, build utility tools and spend time with my family.

I am a photography and music enthusiast with a passion to travel the world. I worry I won’t be able to see all the places I want in this lifetime. But if I had to choose a different lifetime I’d pick the 70s/80s (Some of the best music was created then!!). If I could reset my life and choose a different career, I would love to be a musician or a cinematographer.

I am an aspiring web developer, a promising cook, an explorer, an amateur content creator, a binge watcher, a philomath, a fitness enthusiast, and a lifetime student.

And, I’m still more than these things. I’m better than yesterday. And I will be more things yet to come.
